The Problem

Your tools show data.
Nobody connects the dots.

Amazon and DTC brands run on Seller Central, Vendor Central, Ads Console, Brand Analytics, Unicommerce, Shopify, GA4, and a stack of spreadsheets. When FBA inventory drops, nobody pauses the ads. When fees erode the margin, nobody re-bids. The gap between insight and action is where revenue leaks.

📊

Fragmented data

Seller Central, Vendor Central, Ads Console, Brand Analytics, Unicommerce, Shopify, 3PLs, Excel. Each tool shows a slice. None show your true SKU-level P&L.

🔥

Reactive decisions

You find out after the damage is done. Sponsored ads burned budget on out-of-stock ASINs all weekend. Buy Box was lost on Friday and nobody noticed until Monday.

⏱️

Manual coordination

Your performance marketer doesn't see inventory cover. Your category manager doesn't see ad spend. Every cross-functional Amazon decision takes three people, two days, and a WhatsApp thread.

💸

No margin visibility

SKU-level CM% after FBA fees, referral fees, ad spend, storage, returns, and settlements? Most brands can't compute it without a week of Excel work. By then the bleed is already two weeks old.

How It Works

Data in. Decisions out. Humans govern.

Terminal6 ingests your data, builds a unified brand context, and deploys specialist AI agents that monitor, reason, and act — with full auditability and your rules enforced.

01

Connect your sources

Amazon Seller Central, Vendor Central, Amazon Ads, Shopify, Unicommerce, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and GA4. All normalised into one unified SKU-level data model with your COGS, margins, and brand rules.

02

AI specialists reason like your team

A brand head agent triages every signal each morning, then hands off to specialist managers — category, marketing, channel, operations, finance — who investigate at the SKU level and propose fixes. Every step is reasoned and auditable.

03

You stay in control

Every decision comes with reasoning and a confidence score. Your rules determine what auto-executes and what needs approval. Full audit trail. Kill switches. No black boxes.

AI Employees

Specialist agents that coordinate like a team.

Each agent has a defined scope, its own domain memory, and communicates with other agents before acting. The Growth Agent asks the Supply Agent about restock timelines before increasing bids. We're going Amazon-deep first — Shopify DTC in Phase 2, other marketplaces in Phase 3.

Marketing & Bids

Growth Agent

Inventory-aware ad optimisation across Amazon Ads, Google Ads, and Meta Ads. Throttles bids based on stock cover, margin, and competitive signals. Manages TACOS. Reallocates budget from underperformers to opportunities.

Inventory & Ops

Supply Agent

Predicts stockouts before they happen. Watches FBA cover, lead times, and 3PL signals. Syncs inventory intelligence with the Growth Agent to prevent ad waste and capture demand when stock is healthy.

Pricing & Catalogue

Category Agent

Competitive pricing intelligence. Margin protection after FBA fees, referral fees, and ad spend. Discount calendar management. Identifies assortment gaps and expansion opportunities.

Your Data, Your Control

Your data stays yours.
Never pooled. Never sold.

Terminal6 is a single-tenant analytical tool for the brand that connects it. Data we access through Amazon Selling Partner API, Amazon Advertising API, Vendor Central, Shopify, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and your OMS is used only to power your dashboard, your briefs, and your agents. We do not aggregate data across customers. We do not benchmark you against other brands on the platform. We do not sell, license, or share your marketplace data with anyone.

01

Strictly per-customer

Every record we store is keyed to your brand ID and logically isolated. Our AI agents reason over your data only — they have no access to any other customer's data. No cross-customer training, no cross-customer features, no cross-customer insights.

02

No resale, no redistribution

We never sell, license, or redistribute your Amazon or marketplace data — raw or derived — to any third party. Derived metrics, reports, and recommendations computed on your data are returned only to you and the operators you authorise.

03

Disconnect and delete, any time

You can revoke any connected account in one click. On disconnect, we stop pulling data immediately and delete stored records on request. Full audit trail of every read and every action the agents take on your behalf.

Full details in our Privacy Policy. Terminal6's use of data obtained via the Amazon Selling Partner API complies with the Amazon Acceptable Use Policy.
